Steps for Implementation

In this stage, we work closely with administrators and families to define goals and select the most appropriate technology solutions for the home. This is a collaborative process to ensure the technology meets the needs of all residents.

  • Define objectives
  • Select suitable technology solutions
  • Consult with relevant stakeholders

Our team installs the necessary hardware, including smart screens, VR equipment, and gaming stations. We ensure proper setup and integration with existing infrastructure to guarantee smooth operation.

  • Install smart screens and VR equipment
  • Ensure proper system integration
  • Test hardware functionality

We provide training to both staff and residents to ensure everyone is comfortable with the new technology. The training includes interactive sessions and tutorials for effective usage.

  • Onboarding sessions for staff
  • Training workshops for residents
  • Hands-on tutorials

After the implementation, we continue monitoring the success of the technology, collecting feedback, and making necessary improvements to optimize user experience.

  • Track usage and success metrics
  • Collect feedback from residents and staff
  • Make improvements based on insights
